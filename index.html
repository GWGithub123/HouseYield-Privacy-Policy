Financial Data and Bank Connections

We use Stripe Financial Connections to securely link Your bank account to our Service for the purpose of accessing Your transaction history and financial data.

Data We Access Through Stripe Financial Connections:

When You connect Your bank account through Stripe Financial Connections, we may access and collect:

  • Bank account transaction history (deposits, withdrawals, payments, transfers)
  • Account balance information
  • Account holder name and account details
  • Financial institution information
  • Transaction descriptions, amounts, dates, and categories
  • Merchant names and transaction metadata

How We Use Your Financial Data:

  • To provide bookkeeping and financial management services
  • To categorize and organize Your transactions automatically
  • To generate financial reports, statements, and tax documents
  • To sync transaction data with Your property management accounts
  • To help You track income and expenses for tax purposes
  • To provide insights and analytics about Your financial activity
  • To reconcile accounts and detect potential errors or fraud

Data Security:

Your bank credentials are never stored by Us. Authentication is handled securely by Stripe Financial Connections using bank-level encryption. We only receive read-only access to Your financial data and cannot initiate transactions, move money, or make changes to Your bank accounts.

Your Rights Regarding Financial Data:

You can disconnect Your bank account at any time through the Service settings. Upon disconnection:

  • We will immediately stop accessing new transaction data
  • Historical data may be retained for bookkeeping and tax compliance purposes
  • You can request deletion of all financial data by contacting Us
  • We will delete Your data within 30 days unless we have a legal obligation to retain it

Third-Party Service Provider:

Bank account connections are facilitated by Stripe Financial Connections. Stripe's handling of Your financial data is governed by:

  • Stripe Privacy Policy: https://stripe.com/privacy
  • Stripe Financial Connections Terms: https://stripe.com/legal/financial-connections

Financial transaction data is retained for up to 7 years to comply with IRS tax record-keeping requirements and accounting standards (IRS Publication 583), unless You request earlier deletion and we have no legal obligation to retain it. After this period, financial data is securely deleted.
